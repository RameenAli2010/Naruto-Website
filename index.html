Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto was born in the hidden leaf village shortly before his parents were killed by the 9-tailed fox, which was sealed into Naruto. He had no friends and was in the ninja academy, but couldin't do techniqes correctly, therefore not being able to pass.

Naruto eventually became a ninja and could do many techniques and fought against a villain group called the Akatsuki.
